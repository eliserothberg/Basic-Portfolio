About Me

I love creating things. In addition to coding, I cook and bake, make my own lotions and serums, and sew clothes. I remodeled my condo from the studs up, wrote screenplays, and planned and executed evetns, including a monthly short-film festival.

Figuring out how things work fascinates me: although it's always so much easier to take it apart than put it back together, sadly (sorry, Mom, for never completely reassembling that pinball machine). I'm also fascinated by the relationship of perception and reality; or "the 'airy-fairy' bits of quantum physics", as a friend waggishly put it.
